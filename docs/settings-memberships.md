# Wicket Settings — Memberships

Found under **Wicket → Memberships** in the WordPress admin.

This tab is used to review how Wicket membership tiers map to WooCommerce membership plans, and to configure which WooCommerce product categories are treated as membership products.

---

## Membership Configuration Overview

A summary table that displays all Wicket Account Membership Tiers alongside their corresponding WooCommerce Membership Plans. This gives you a quick way to confirm that the integration between Wicket and WooCommerce is set up correctly.

If you have recently made changes to your Wicket Membership Tiers, or if you have switched between Wicket Environments (staging/production), the table may be out of date. Use the refresh button on this section to clear the cached list and reload the latest data from Wicket.

---

## Membership Settings

### Membership Categories

A multi-select field that lets you choose which WooCommerce product categories contain membership products. Marking categories here allows other parts of the plugin — and custom development — to identify which products are memberships. This selection also feeds into settings such as hiding membership product pages and filtering them out of search results (configured in the Integrations tab).
